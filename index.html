Five reasons to subscribe to Kyiv Post

Your Digital Subscription helps us stay independent and focus on quality reporting

Why support us? Here's our simple pitch to you:

We are the only English-language newspaper in Ukraine

The Kyiv Post turned 20 this year and is the only English-language newspaper in Ukraine that managed to survive and raise several generations of talented journalists. Your suport helped us became Ukraine's global voice in English-speaking world. We are doing our best to deliver honest news about Ukraine's reality. Most of our exclusive content is free and stay free for up to 24 hours. We kindly ask you contribute and support production of reliable news.

We withstand Russian propaganda

At a time when Ukraine is being outgunned in the information war by Kremlin propaganda, the Kyiv Post matters more than ever as a source of reliable English-language news. Citations in the Western press: More than any other source of news in Ukraine and just behind one other publication even when Russia is taken into account. Please help us keep up the good work.

We are one of a few independent media outlets in Ukraine

A vibrant press is essential to any democracy – especially Ukraine's fledgling state and attempts to shed its Soviet past. The Kyiv Post is of a few media outlets that managed to preserve its independent status in a nation where too many news outlets are still owned by business moguls who use their holdings as propaganda tools. The Kyiv Post's courageous journalism has led to several threats to its existence – including two failed attempts by oligarchs close to ex-President Viktor Yanukovych to purchase the newspaper; a failed libel lawsuit by billionaire Dmytro Firtash, who is now facing criminal bribery charges that he denies; and a demand by a Yanukovych-era agricultural minister to fire the chief editor for publishing a hard-hitting interview with him. Your subscription will help us stay in business during these harsh times.

Great journalism requires community support.

We try to raise the bar for quality reporting in Ukraine

The Kyiv Post matters as a role-model of ethical journalism with Western standards in Ukraine. The newspaper adheres to the principle of the business - editorial divide. Commercial team, as well as our publisher, have no influence on editorial editorial decisions. Our journalists also appear regularly at Ukraine's journalism schools to offer lectures, training and advice.

Media need a sustainable business model

Production of reliable news is a costly business. Do you know who pays for your information online? The Internet disrupted the core of print business, significantly lowering prices for ads. Inflation of banner ad prices has put a lot of media organizations out of business. We can't rely on ads anymore. As the only news organization in Ukraine that erected a paywall, we believe that subscription and micropayments represent a sustainable business model for news media.
